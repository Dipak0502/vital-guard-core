# 🛡️ VitalGuard – Emergency Medical Information System

> **Your medical information, available when it matters most.**

VitalGuard is a secure emergency medical information platform that enables users to store their own and their family members' critical medical information in one place. In case of an emergency, such as an accident or sudden illness, healthcare professionals can instantly access life-saving information through a secure Emergency QR Code, reducing treatment delays and improving patient outcomes.

🌐 **Live Demo:** https://vital-guard-core.lovable.app

---

## 📖 Overview

Every second counts during a medical emergency. Patients are often unconscious, unable to communicate, or separated from their family members. VitalGuard bridges this gap by providing instant access to critical medical information such as:

- Blood Group
- Allergies
- Current Medications
- Chronic Diseases
- Emergency Contacts
- Medical History

The platform is designed with **privacy-first principles**, ensuring users control what information is shared during emergencies.
